Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that your entire family will love...even your cat!

Whether its meat-based or vegetarian, you can modify this classic lasagna recipe to satisfy your tastes.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of no-boil Lasagna Noodles
  • 24 oz Tomato Sauce
  • 16 oz Italian Sausage or Vegetables
  • 16 oz Ricotta Cheese
  • 24 oz Moozzarella Cheese
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Mix the eggs, Ricotta, Parmesan, and 8 oz of Mozzarella cheese.
  3. Brown the meat or saute vegetables.
  4. Assemble lasagna by layering noodles, cheese mixture, and meat.
  5. Once assembly is complete, pour remaining sauce on top and cover with Mozzarella cheese.
  6. Bake for 35 minutes covered with foil. Then remove for last 5 minutes.
  7. Let lasagna sit for 10 minutes before serving.
