Overnight Oats

Description

Overnight oats are a great way to start your day off.

Make these the night before, as the name implies, and save yourself time in the morning!

Ingredients

  • 6 oz oats
  • 6 oz Almond or Soy Milk
  • Dried fruit of your choice
  • Almonds or Walnuts

Steps

  1. Simply mix all the ingredients in a glass bowl with cover.
  2. Place in refirgerator and allow to sit for at least 8 hours.
  3. Enjoy in the morning with fresh fruit on top.
