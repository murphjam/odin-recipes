Salad

Description

Salad makes a great and light lunch.

This recipe can be easily modified or follow the steps for a low-calorie lunch plan.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz fresh greens, such as kale, spinach, or arugala
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Sliced cucumber
  • Slivered carrots
  • Salad dressing of your choice.

Steps

  1. Mix all the vegetables together.
  2. Add dressing on top.
